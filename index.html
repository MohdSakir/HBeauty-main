Hemal Rawat is the MD of JSR Group, a diversified group with interests in Hospitality, real estate and film production. She has been instrumental in transforming JSR Group into one of India's leading conglomerates and now she has launched H Beauty Studio with the hopes of taking it to new heights. Her passion has driven her to create unforgettable experiences for her guests, and she believes in providing best-in-class service standards to everyone who walks through the doors of H Beauty Studio.
The H Beauty Studio brings you the freshest services to enhance your beauty. We believe beauty is inclusive; everyone deserves to feel beautiful and pampered. With top-of-the-line products, exquisite amenities, and expertly educated staff, our studio is a pioneer in offering consumers a superb experience for hair, skin, hands & feet, and makeup. Even though you are busy, taking care of ourselves is crucial. It is as essential to get massages and be well-groomed as it is to eat healthy food, exercise, and get enough sleep.
